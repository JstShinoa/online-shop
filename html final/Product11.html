NZXT H5 Flow (CC-H51FB-01) Tempered Glass ATX Mid Tower Chassis (Black)

₱4,350.00

The H5 Flow features a perforated front panel to generate an extra dimension of airflow for maximum cooling potential. With optimized thermal performance and intuitive cable management, the H5 Flow is an ideal chassis for most builds.

Specifications:

  • The PSU shroud is perforated on the side and bottom, enabling optimal air intake from two 120mm fans (not included).
  • Supports up to a 360mm radiator in front and a 240mm radiator up top for powerful liquid cooling potential.
  • Equipped with two 120mm Quiet Airflow fans (CV) —one in front, one in rear—for solid out-of-the-box performance.
  • Effortlessly route and conceal cables using the wide channels, hooks, and straps.
